How to open ONLINE BUS REGISTRATION SYSTEM.
1.	Install winrar on your pc.
2.	Un compress ONLINE bus registration folder on your desktop.
3.	Using wampp server, start apache and mysql.
4.	Open the folder you had installed your wampserver, mostly in LOCAL disk C:
5.	Open www C:\wamp64\www folder and Copy paste Online bus reg files except the files named DATABASE.
6.	Go back to the root folder “Wamp64” and open C:\wamp64\bin\mysql\mysql8.0.21\data and paste online_bus_registration_system.
